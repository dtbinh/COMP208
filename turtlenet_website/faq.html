Frequently Asked Questions

What is Turtletnet?

Turtlenet is an encrypted social media network.

Why does it need to be encrypted?

Privacy and the internet is becoming a very large, contentious, issue in the past few years. In order to make sure your communications arent read by those unauthorised, the safest bet is to make sure your communications are encrypted in some way.

How is it encrypted?

We use a combination of both RSA and AES encryption for communications.

Aren't people monitored just for speaking to the wrong person?

Turtlenet has got you covered. Not only is your data encrypted it's also anonymous so nobody can see who you've been communicating with.

Can I share photos and videos?

We do not support the sharing of images and videos via this service, as it would create extremely large transfers of data once encrypted. As broadband infrastructure increases in speed, it may become a viable option in the future.

Can't I just encrypt my facebook/twitter/other social media?

No, unfortunately those social media networks do not offer this as an option, as it would decrease their overall revenue drastically.

How much does it cost?

We at the Turtlenet Project do not believe privacy should be paid for, it should be respected. Turtlenet is completely free to use and do with as you please, it is completely open source.

Whats with the turtle?

Turtles are awesome, do not question turtles.
