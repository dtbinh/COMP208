Who are we?

We are the makers of Turtlenet, we are Ballmer Peak.

"to shift societal norms to a state wherein privacy is respected without caveat or justification"

Secure

We at Ballmer Peak believe in securing online data without the need for justification. A user should not have to justify why data is encrypted any more than they would be asked to justify why they don't want cameras in their home. Currently however pepople may are regarded as suspicious merely for being in possession of encrypted data, a situation we regard as unjust.

Free

We believe that software should be developed in the open. Our development infrastructure and communication channels are public, and our code can be freely downloaded, modified and shared with others. All our contributors have the same rights.

Fair

With large government organisations recording large swathes of the internet in recent years, it seems only the only just way forward is to stymie governmental effors at mass surveilance and so remove innocent citizens from constant surveilance previously reserved for criminals. If encryption like this can be available for everyone to use, it will allow all to enjoy freedom of expression regardless of the popularity their views.
