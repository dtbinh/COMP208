Who are we?

We are the makers of Turtlenet, we are Ballmer Peak.

"to shift societal norms to a state wherein privacy is respected without caveat or justification"

Secure

We at Ballmer Peak believe in securing online data without the need for justification. A user should not have to justify why data is encrypted, only that it is. Currently however a user can be regarded as suspicious if they are in possession of encrypted data, a situation we regard as unjust.

Free

We believe that software should be developed in the open. Our development infrastructure and communication channels are public, and our code can be freely downloaded, modified and shared with others. All our contributors have the same rights.

Fair

With large government organisations recording large swathes of the internet in recent years, it seems only the only fair way forward is to keep your data encrypted. If encryption like this can be available for even novices to use, it will create a greater level of equality between users of the internet.
